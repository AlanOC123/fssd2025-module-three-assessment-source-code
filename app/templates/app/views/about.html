Welcome to Planet Stop

Planet Stop is a simple, fast way to learn about our Solar System. Browse the planets, follow a short history of spaceflight, and check what you remember with a quick quiz.

What you can do

Explore: clear, bite-size facts and images for each planet.

Timeline: key moments that shaped space exploration.

Quiz: build a short quiz and get instant feedback.

How it feels

Simple: clean pages, clear headings, easy to navigate.

Fast: lightweight images, quick loading on mobile and desktop.

Accessible: readable text, high contrast, and keyboard-friendly controls.

Privacy at a glance

No sign-up and no downloads. Your quiz progress can be kept during your visit, but nothing personal is stored.
